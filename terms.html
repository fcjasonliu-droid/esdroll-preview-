    1. Trading Terms

    All quotations are issued in USD under FOB Shanghai or FOB Ningbo Incoterms 2020, unless otherwise specified in writing. Prices are valid for 30 days from the date of issue and subject to raw material index adjustments beyond that period.

    2. Payment Terms

    New clients: 30% T/T deposit upon order confirmation, 70% balance against copy of B/L.

    Established clients (3+ orders): Open to negotiate L/C at sight, O/A 30 days, or other terms upon credit review.

    3. Shipping & Lead Time

    Standard sizes in stock: 7–10 days to FOB port.

    Custom orders: 15–25 days production + 3–5 days export documentation. Express sample shipments via DHL / FedEx at buyer's account.

    4. Warranty

    All ESD mats carry a 2-year limited warranty against manufacturing defects under proper storage and usage conditions. Warranty does not cover mechanical damage, chemical exposure outside product spec, or improper grounding.

    5. Returns & Claims

    Claims for visible defects must be filed within 7 days of receipt with photographic evidence. Functional or specification claims must be filed within 30 days. Approved returns are subject to a 15% restocking fee for non-defective goods.

    We collect only the information you voluntarily provide via our contact and quote forms: name, email, company, phone, country, and message content. This data is used solely to respond to your inquiry, prepare quotations, and arrange shipments.

    We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties except as required for shipping (courier companies, freight forwarders) or legal compliance. You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data at any time by emailing coco@esdroll.com.
