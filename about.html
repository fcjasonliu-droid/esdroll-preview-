    About EDAR

    25+ years of ESD material science — factory-direct from Suzhou, Anhui, China, exported to 50+ countries.

    From Workshop to Industry Leader

    Founded in 1998, Suzhou Yida Rubber & Plastic Technology Co., Ltd. (宿州市亿达橡塑科技有限公司) has grown from a small Suzhou workshop into one of China's most trusted ESD mat manufacturers. Today our factory covers 18,000 m² with 30+ dedicated production lines, including rubber compounding, calendaring, vulcanization, and die-cutting — every step under one roof.

    Our products — general ESD mats, RoHS 2.0/3.0 compliant mats, 2-layer and 3-layer anti-fatigue mats, and ESD PVC curtains — are exported to 50+ countries across North America, Europe, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East. We serve semiconductor fabs, EMS providers, repair workshops, and OEM/ODM customers.

    What sets us apart is factory-direct pricing without middleman markups, batch-to-batch consistency verified by 8-point electrical testing, and full TDS/MSDS documentation with every order. From raw rubber to finished mat, we control the entire process — so you get predictable quality at predictable cost.
